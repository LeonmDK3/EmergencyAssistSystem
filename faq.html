Frequently asked questions

EAS+ instantly shuts down or I get some weird error in console that I don't understand.

Make sure that you have your username sert in the settings.txt file "key=username". Also make sure you have .net Framework 4.7 installed.

My speedometer and RPM don't show anything.

At the heart of EAS+ is a modern minimap that provides you with critical information during chases. With a trail behind the suspect, you can keep up with high-speed chases and make split-second decisions to keep them in your sights. The minimap also shows the location of pursuing police cars when playing as a civilian, enabling you to plan your escape more strategically and avoid getting caught.

Advanced MiniMap

Make sure you set OutGauge Mode 1, OutGauge Delay 1, OutGauge Port 30001

I can't see the minimap when I'm in game

Make sure you are running the game in Borderless Fullscreen mode (SHIFT + F11) or check your other monitor if you have more than 1
