EAS+: The Ultimate Tool for [TC] City Driving Server

Are you looking for a tool that can help you become a skilled driver and perform your role to the best of your ability on the City Driving server? Look no further than EAS+! With its impressive features and easy-to-use interface, EAS+ is a must-have application for any serious LFS player on the City Driving server. Take your driving to the next level and stay ahead of the competition with EAS+.

Advanced MiniMap

At the heart of EAS+ is a modern minimap that provides you with critical information during chases. With a trail behind the suspect, you can keep up with high-speed chases and make split-second decisions to keep them in your sights. The minimap also shows the location of pursuing police cars when playing as a civilian, enabling you to plan your escape more strategically and avoid getting caught.

Fully customizable HUD message box

EAS+ includes on-screen buttons to quickly send chat messages to your teammates and a role-dependent HUD that displays role-specific information for cop, medic, rescue, and tow truck drivers.

Automated Messages

During chases, you can send automated messages to let the cop team know what's happening. An automated message will also be sent during chases to remind players to keep a safe distance from the chase, improving the overall gameplay experience for all players.

Police/Civ HUD

With a digital speedometer and RPM gauge, integrated siren and police lights, and suspect position HUD showing an arrow pointing to the suspect direction and distance, EAS+ provides all the information you need to stay ahead of the competition.

Automation

Included is a automated siren when starting/ending a chase. There is also an auto engine start when pressing the gas pedal for a quicker take off. Automatic headlights and it even allows for auto speed trap sharing, further enhancing the immersive experience on the City Driving server.

Much More

Numpad binds allow you to access important features and commands without having to navigate through menus. Chat logs make it easy to review past conversations, and the car contact feature allows you to share your contact details with other players. The immersive cop chatter and police chase music can also be toggled on and off easily.
